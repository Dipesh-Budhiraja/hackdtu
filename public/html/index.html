About Us

The Perfect Solution To Your Travel Hassles


We at Eco-Ride bring to you the very first 'Mobility as a Service' prototype that attempts to change the way we travel n NCR. The need for a personal vehicle does not exist anymore, no more worries of extended warranties or car repair services! Get your ride from Vroom and drive or be driven to anywhere and everywhere. Moreover, we're paving way for more livable cities and better public spaces.

Our Goal


Digital Movement-Urban Convenience: The primary target that we plan to achieve is the incoming of a new consumer-transport relationship. The process of commuting is one click away and the only things you need are an Eco-Ride subcription and a few personal details that we require to get you your best ride. We truly believe that a full-fledged vehicle rental system can urbanise our transport and make travel convenient.


Reduced Congestion-Cleaner Air: The Eco-Ride initiative also benefits the system through better public spaces, reduced recurrent and non-recurrent congestion on busy routes and a more eco-friendly travel approach that ultimately saves your time and money.
